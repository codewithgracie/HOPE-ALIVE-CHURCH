Beliefs and History

Burning Voices Ministries (Hope Alive Church Intl) is founded on the Word of God. A Christian should live for the glory of God and the well-being of others, sharing His love with all nations.

A Christian should live for the glory of God and the well-being of others.

At Burning Voices Ministries, we believe in sharing God's love through faith, compassion, and service, reaching hearts and transforming lives in our community and beyond.

Welcome to Hope Alive Church

Connect, Grow and Serve with Us

The Burning Voices Ministries a. k.a Hope Alive Church is an offshoot of the Church of Jesus Christ. It is established as Watchmen upon the walls of this great City; Abuja, Nigeria, while declaring the Word of Salvation and Hope therein to the nations of Africa and nations across the world, resulting to overflowing prosperity upon His cities (Nations). Jeremiah 31:6, Zechariah- 1:17

Be part of a welcoming community at Burning Voices Ministries, where you can connect with others who share your faith, grow through worship, study, and fellowship, and serve with purpose and compassion. Together, we impact lives, spread God's love, and build a stronger, hope-filled community both locally and beyond.

What We Believe

Our Foundation

We believe in the authority of Scripture and the power of God's Word to transform lives. Our faith is centered on Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior, and we are committed to sharing His message of salvation with all.

Our Mission

As watchmen upon the walls of Abuja and beyond, we are dedicated to declaring the Word of Salvation and Hope to the nations of Africa and across the world, bringing prosperity and transformation to His cities and peoples.

Burning Voice Ministry

Guided by God's Word, Burning Voice Ministry is dedicated to spreading hope, sharing salvation, and nurturing a community of faith where lives are uplifted and hearts are transformed.
