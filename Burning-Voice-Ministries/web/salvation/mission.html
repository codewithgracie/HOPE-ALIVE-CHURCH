Mission

Watchmen of Hope (Jeremiah 31:6, Zechariah 1:17)

At Burning Voices Ministries Inc. a.k.a Hope Alive Church Int'l, we are called to stand as watchmen upon the walls of Abuja, Nigeria, declaring the Word of salvation and prosperity with hope to all nations. Our mandate is to lift up the banner of Christ in this generation interceding for our city and our land while proclaiming God's truth across Africa and to the ends of the earth. “Watchmen of Hope” reflects our divine assignment: to be a people who guard, pray, and declare God's promises until His glory covers the earth. We believe that through His Word, the broken will be restored, nations will prosper, and countless lives will encounter the hope found only in Jesus Christ.

Proclaiming Hope to the Nations (Zechariah 1:17)

At Burning Voices Ministries Inc. a.k.a Hope Alive Church Int''l, our mission is to be a voice of truth in our generation declaring salvation, prosperity, and hope from Abuja to the ends of the earth. We are called to lift up the Word of God as a beacon of light in a world filled with darkness, standing as watchmen who speak life over cities and nations. “Proclaiming Hope to the Nations” is the heartbeat of our ministry. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we are raising men and women who carry the message of Christ beyond borders, bringing healing to the broken, strength to the weary, and prosperity to the desolate places. We believe that as this hope is declared, God''s glory will spread across Africa and to every nation of the world.

Watchmen on the Walls (Isaiah 62:6-7)

At Burning Voices Ministries Inc. a.k.a Hope Alive Church Int'l, our calling is to stand as watchmen upon the walls of Abuja, lifting up the banner of Christ and declaring His Word of salvation, hope, and prosperity to all nations. We believe God has placed us as spiritual gatekeepers interceding for our city, our nation, and the world, until His glory fills the earth. “Watchmen on the Walls” is more than a theme; it is our divine assignment. As watchmen, we sound the alarm of salvation, proclaim hope to the hopeless, and announce the coming of God's abundance to the nations. Through prayer, preaching, and the power of the Holy Spirit, we are raising men and women who will carry this light to every generation and every land.

Declaring Salvation to the Nations (Mark 16:15)

At Burning Voices Ministries Inc. a.k.a Hope Alive Church Int'l, we are watchmen upon the walls of Abuja, Nigeria, declaring the message of Christ to every tribe, tongue, and nation. Our mission is to preach the Word of salvation, bringing hope to the hopeless and light to those in darkness. “Declaring Salvation to the Nations” reminds us that the gospel is God's power to save, transform, and prosper lives. We are committed to raising a people who will carry this message beyond borders, until the nations are filled with the knowledge of Christ..

The Hope of the Nations (Colossians 1:27)

At Burning Voices Ministries Inc. a.k.a Hope Alive Church Int'l, our calling is to proclaim Christ as the eternal hope for Abuja, for Nigeria, and for the nations of the world. As watchmen, we stand to intercede, to declare His promises, and to spread the good news of salvation and prosperity. “The Hope of the Nations” is our declaration that in Christ, broken lives are restored, nations are lifted, and the future is secured. Through the power of His Word and the Spirit, we shine forth His glory, carrying hope to generations yet to come.

Burning Voice Ministry

Guided by God's Word, Burning Voice Ministry is dedicated to spreading hope, sharing salvation, and nurturing a community of faith where lives are uplifted and hearts are transformed.
